Read and follow the workflow at `workflows/dev-workflow/WORKFLOW.md`.
It defines the full development pipeline — skills, review tools, references,
and conventions. All workflow content lives there.

# Hard Rules

- Do not add dependencies unless there is a clear need.
- Do not refactor unrelated files while implementing a task.
- Keep instructions deterministic and actionable.
- Prefer bounded changes over broad rewrites.

# Standard Completion Checklist

- Relevant files are updated and internally consistent.
- Validation steps are documented in the response.
- Risks, assumptions, and follow-up items are explicitly called out.
